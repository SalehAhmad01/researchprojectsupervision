Upload rules

01 Existing usernames are updated

If a username already exists, the account details are refreshed from the CSV.

02 IDs are role-aware

Students use Student ID. Supervisors use Staff ID.

03 Eligibility controls assignment

Use eligible=true for students who can be assigned to project supervision.

04 Departments support matching

Upload supervisors with departments so coordinators can assign by academic area.
